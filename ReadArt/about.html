About Us

ReadArt Africa is more than a platform - it's a movement. We exist to celebrate African storytelling and creativity, making local books and accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our story

We started with one idea: African literature and art deserve a bigger stage. Many stories go untold, many artists unseen. ReadArt Africa bridges that gap by giving readers and creators a digital home to connect, share, and grow.


Every painting, poem, and page here carries a heartbeat. We believe that creativity is a form of activism - a way to reimagine our world through culture, color, and conversation.

Our Mission

To empower readers and artists by celebrating creativity, preserving culture, and promoting access to knowledge.

Our Impact

We link to global goals: quality education, sustainable communities, and cultural pride. Every book read and artwork seen makes a difference.

Join Us

Be part of our journey. Explore our library, submit your art, or simply share our vision. Together, we shape Africa's creative future.
